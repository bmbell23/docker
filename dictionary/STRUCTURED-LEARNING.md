# Structured Language Learning System

## 🎓 Complete Curriculum-Based Learning!

Your dictionary now has a **comprehensive, structured learning system** with proper curriculum, lessons, quizzes, and progress tracking!

## 📚 Learning Structure

### 20 Levels × 100 Words = 2,000 Words Total

**Level System:**
- **Level 1**: 100 most common words (the, be, to, of, and, etc.)
- **Level 2**: Next 100 common words
- **Level 3-5**: Progressively more advanced vocabulary
- **Levels 6-20**: Coming soon!

**Each Level Contains:**
- **10 Lessons** (10 words per lesson)
- **Comprehensive quizzes** every 10 words
- **Level test** after completing all 100 words

### 3. Take a Lesson (Teaching Phase)

Each lesson teaches 10 words with:

**For Each Word:**
- ✅ **Word** - The English word
- ✅ **Pronunciation** - Phonetic guide (e.g., /həˈloʊ/)
- ✅ **Part of Speech** - Noun, verb, adjective, etc.
- ✅ **Definition** - Clear, concise meaning
- ✅ **Example Sentence** - Word used in context
- ✅ **Audio** - Listen to pronunciation (when available)

**Example Lesson Card:**
```
1. hello
/həˈloʊ/
[interjection]

Used as a greeting or to begin a phone conversation.

"Hello, how are you today?"

🔊 Listen
```

### 4. Take the Quiz (Practice Phase)

After studying the lesson:
- **10 questions** - One for each word
- **Multiple choice** - 4 options per question
- **Instant feedback** - Green for correct, red for wrong
- **Correct answer shown** - Learn from mistakes
- **Stats tracked** - Score, total, streak

**Quiz Format:**
```
What is "hello" in Spanish?

hello

[ ] Adiós
[ ] Por favor
[ ] Hola      ← Correct!
[ ] Gracias
```

### 5. Track Your Progress

**Three Word States:**
1. **📚 Learned** - Seen in a lesson (blue)
2. **📝 Practiced** - Quizzed at least once (yellow)
3. **⭐ Mastered** - 3+ correct answers (green)

**Progress View Shows:**
- Total words learned
- Total words practiced
- Total words mastered
- All words organized by status
