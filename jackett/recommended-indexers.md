# Recommended Jackett Indexers (Alternatives to The Pirate Bay)

## Public Indexers (No Registration Required)

### Most Reliable:
1. **1337x** - Very stable, good content variety
2. **RARBG** - High-quality releases (if still available)
3. **YTS** - Movies only, excellent quality
4. **EZTV** - TV shows focused
5. **Torlock** - Good general content
6. **LimeTorrents** - Reliable alternative
7. **TorrentGalaxy** - Growing popularity, good content

### Additional Options:
- **Zooqle** - Good for movies and TV
- **Torrentz2** - Meta-search engine
- **ExtraTorrent** - General content
- **KickassTorrents** - Various mirrors available

## Semi-Public Indexers (Easy Registration)

1. **TorrentLeech** - High quality, fast speeds
2. **AlphaRatio** - Good general content
3. **Nebulance** - TV and movies focused

## Setup Instructions

1. **Access Jackett:** http://100.123.154.40:9117
2. **Add Indexers:**
   - Click "Add indexer"
   - Search for indexer name
   - Click "+" to add
   - Configure if needed (most public ones need no config)
3. **Test Each Indexer:**
   - Click the wrench icon next to each indexer
   - Click "Test" to verify it's working
4. **Copy API URLs:**
   - Use the Torznab Feed URLs in your torrent client

## Pro Tips

- **Add multiple indexers** for better search coverage
- **Test regularly** as indexers can go offline
- **Use both general and specialized indexers** (e.g., YTS for movies, EZTV for TV)
- **Check indexer status** in Jackett's dashboard regularly
